A Business Card With Actual Powers (Sort Of)

Sure, your card looks cool. But it also does things.

Every Legendary Profile includes a built‑in QR code that acts like your personal digital portal. Scan it, and boom—your contact info pops right into someone’s phone. No typing. No awkward “how do you spell that again.” No excuses.
